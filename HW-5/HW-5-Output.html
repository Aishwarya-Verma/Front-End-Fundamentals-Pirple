Making Easy Pancakes

Hello, my name is Aishwarya Verma. I'm going to share an easy recipe on making easy pancakes.
Pancakes are light and very tasty. So let's start making them!

Ingredients:

  • 100g Plain flour
  • Two Large Eggs
  • 300ml Milk
  • 1 tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • Lemon Wedges (To Serve)
  • Caster Sugar (To Serve)

Steps to Cook:

  1. Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tablespoon vegetable oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl, then whisk to a smooth batter. 🥣
  2. Let aside for 30 mins to rest. 🕜
  3. Set a medium frying pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper. ♨
  4. When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden, keeping them warm in a low oven as you go. 🥮
  5. Serve with lemon wedges and caster sugar, or your favourite filling. 🥘

    6. Once cold, you can layer the pancakes between baking parchment, then wrap in cling film and freeze for up to 2 months. 😁
