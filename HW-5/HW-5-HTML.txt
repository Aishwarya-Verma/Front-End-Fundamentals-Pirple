  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>  
  <!--List of Ingredients-->
  <ul class="ingredients">
    <li><em>100g</em> Plain flour</li>
    <li><em>Two</em> Large Eggs</li>
    <li><em>300ml</em> Milk</li>
    <li><em>1 tablespoon </em>Vegetable Oil</li>
    <li>Lemon Wedges <em>(To Serve)</em></li>
    <li>Caster Sugar <em>(To Serve)</em></li>
   </ul>
  <!--Steps to cook recipe-->
   <h3>Steps to Cook:</h3>
  <ol class="steps">
    <li>Put 100g plain flour, 
      2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tablespoon vegetable oil 
      and a pinch of salt into a bowl, 
      then whisk to a smooth batter. &#x1F963</li>
    <li>Let aside for 30 mins to rest. &#x1F55C</li>
    <li>Set a medium frying pan over a medium heat and 
      carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper. &#x2668</li>
    <li>When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden, 
      keeping them warm in a low oven as you go. &#x1F96E</li>
    <li>Serve with lemon wedges and caster sugar, 
      or your favourite filling. &#x1F958</li>
      <p><em>Once cold, you can layer the pancakes between baking parchment, 
      then wrap in cling film and freeze for up to 2 months.</em> &#x1F601</p>
  </ol>
